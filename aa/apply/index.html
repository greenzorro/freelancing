Apply Now

We Make It Simple.

Applying to the American Access program takes about one hour.

 

You will need the following items:

  • Your secondary school transcript and a certified English translation if your original transcript is not in English.
  • If you have graduated already, include your secondary school diploma and a certified English translation if your original diploma is not in English.

Click the button below to start your application. You can save your progress and continue at a later time if needed. Have questions about the application process? Please contact us.

If you are working with an agent or educational counselor, please submit your application through them.

Start Application

Once you submit your application, an American Access representative will contact you with instructions on next steps.
